Postmortems

Blameless incident retrospectives with timelines, root causes, and action items.

Postmortems are blameless. Their purpose is to understand what happened, why it happened, and what systemic changes will prevent recurrence. They are not for assigning fault.

See templates/postmortem-template.md for the standard format.
