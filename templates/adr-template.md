## Context

What is the situation that necessitates this decision? What forces are at play — technical
constraints, team capabilities, business requirements, prior decisions? Describe the
problem space, not the solution.

## Decision

What have we decided to do? State the decision clearly and directly.

## Consequences

### Positive
- What becomes easier or better as a result of this decision?

### Negative
- What becomes harder, more expensive, or is accepted as a known trade-off?

### Neutral / follow-on work
- What other decisions or work items does this decision create or defer?
